Spaghetti and Mince

Description

Spaghetti and Mince is always a winner for a quick and easy but very filling dish. It is usually enjoyed as a hot meal at dinner but can be enjoyed for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of mince
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese (any cheese will do)
